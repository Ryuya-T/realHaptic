\begin{abstract}
  In order to extend the dynamic range of the force/torque sensor, a high dynamic range force measuring device that uses both a semiconductor strain gauge and a general strain gauge was developed. It was experimentally shown that the dynamic range can be extended without complicating the structure of the strain body.
\end{abstract}
